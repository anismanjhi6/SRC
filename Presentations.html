PRESENTATIONS

Deadlines!! : The deadline for submissions of abstracts is 28th January, 2017! Undergraduate and master’s students are invited to present their original/semi-original research and technical projects in the form of oral or poster presentations in the following fourteen domains.

Computer Science Engineering

Electrical Engineering

Mathematics

Physics

Chemistry

Economics

Civil Engineering

Aerospace Engineering

Metallurgy and Material Science

Mechanical

Design

Biotech and Bioengineering

Industrial Management

Chemical

Teams comprising of less than 5 members can register for the presentation by sending an abstract, maximum of one page, of their research project. Abstract should contain the motivation for the research problem, the solution approach and final results and conclusions. Teams can opt for oral or poster presentation as per their choice during the registration. Based on the abstracts some teams will be finalized for oral and poster presentations. After the declaration of results of abstract selection, invited students will have to register for presentations. Registration fee is ₹500/- (300/- accommodation charge + 200/- security deposit) per person. On the basis of abstracts, travel grants will be provided of maximum ₹ 2000 for a 3-tier air-conditioned Railway ticket or below for the top 42 entries, 3 from each department. Participants will be provided accommodation in IIT Kanpur.
